# Author Brand & Platform Strategy

## Overview

Our Author Brand & Platform Strategy service helps authors build the audience and identity that sell books over the long term — not a single launch campaign, but a durable platform. We develop your author brand (positioning, voice, and visual identity), grow and activate the channels you own (newsletter, social, and website), and turn an engaged following into a reliable base of readers and buyers. This service complements our Marketing & Promotion and Author Website services by focusing on the foundational strategy: who you are to your readers, and how you reach them directly.

## Core Offerings

Author Brand Definition: We define your author positioning, voice, and visual identity — the consistent throughline that ties your books, your channels, and your public presence together.

Owned-Channel Strategy: A plan for the channels you control — primarily your newsletter, website, and social presence — designed to build a direct, high-converting relationship with readers rather than renting attention.

Audience Growth Plan: Concrete tactics to grow your list and following: lead magnets, reader funnels, collaborations, and content cadence tuned to your genre and goals.

Platform Activation: Turning an audience into buyers: launch sequences, evergreen reader journeys, and the rhythm of communication that keeps a following engaged between releases.

## Optional Add-Ons

Newsletter Setup & Growth: Setup or migration of your email platform, list-building mechanics, and a content plan to lift open and conversion rates.

Social Content Systems: Repeatable content frameworks and templates so maintaining a presence is sustainable rather than overwhelming.

Brand Style Guide: A concise guide to your author brand — voice, tone, colors, and typography — so everything you publish stays consistent.

Audience Analytics & Reporting: Tracking the metrics that matter — list growth, engagement, and conversion — with periodic reporting and recommendations.

## Ideal For

Authors building a long-term career across multiple books who want a durable audience, not just one launch.

Authors with a manuscript or catalog but little direct audience who need to build a platform from the ground up.

Authors with a following on rented channels who want to convert it into owned, direct relationships.

Authors who want their brand to be coherent across books, website, newsletter, and social.

## Deliverables

An author brand definition: positioning, voice, and visual identity with a concise style guide.

An owned-channel and audience-growth strategy with a concrete content and activation plan.

Configured or optimized newsletter and social systems ready to run.

An analytics framework and reporting cadence to track audience growth and conversion over time.
