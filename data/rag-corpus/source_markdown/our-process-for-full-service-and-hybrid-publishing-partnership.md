# Full-Service & Hybrid Publishing Partnership Process

A partnership should feel decisive, not difficult. We begin with research and a recommendation, agree the model and terms transparently, and then move into structured execution and growth.

Catalog Review & Findings: Conduct a title-by-title review of the catalog's performance and platform data, and summarize the findings and the opportunity.

Model Recommendation: Recommend Full-Service or Hybrid-Assisted based on the catalog, the author's goals, and the role of institutional or specialty sales — with a side-by-side comparison.

Terms & Agreement: Walk through the publishing structure transparently — production expectations, distribution support, revenue model, and where responsibility sits on each side — before anything is signed.

Implementation Roadmap: Set a phased roadmap with milestones for transition, optimization, and launches.

Growth Execution: Execute the title-by-title growth plan, optimize the backlist, and launch in-development titles into the established catalog.

Review & Renew: Report on performance against the plan and review the partnership at agreed intervals.
