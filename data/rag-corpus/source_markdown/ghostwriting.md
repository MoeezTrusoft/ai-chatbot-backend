## The BookCraft Ghostwriting Process

Our collaborative process turns your vision into a professionally written manuscript, step by step:

1. **Vision mapping.** We begin with a detailed consultation to discuss your concept, goals, target audience, tone, and inspirations. Whether you have a spark of an idea, a detailed outline, or a half-finished draft, we help shape your vision into a solid plan. **Outcome:** clear objectives, a project roadmap, and a ghostwriting game plan tailored to you.

2. **Strategic story structuring.** We develop a custom blueprint for your book — chapter outline, narrative arc (for fiction) or argument structure (for non-fiction), themes, structure, and flow appropriate to your genre. **Outcome:** a strategic content outline that aligns with your vision.

3. **Ghostwriter match & commencement.** We pair you with a writer who specializes in your genre and style. In a kickoff meeting, you connect with your writer to share your voice and preferences directly. NDAs are in place from the start. **Outcome:** the writing process is underway with a ghostwriter who "gets" you.

4. **Chapter-by-chapter drafts.** Instead of waiting for a full manuscript, we share work in progress with you in stages. You receive chapters (or sections) to review regularly. This iterative approach ensures the tone, content, and style stay on track. **Outcome:** ongoing collaboration — you see your book taking shape and can give feedback throughout.

5. **Feedback & revisions.** Your input is invaluable. We invite you to comment on each draft, and we revise accordingly. Whether it's restructuring a scene, adjusting voice, or adding details, we refine the manuscript until it exceeds your expectations. **Outcome:** a manuscript that feels authentically yours, perfected through collaborative refinement.

6. **Final manuscript delivery.** Once all chapters are written and approved, we perform a comprehensive review and polish. The completed manuscript is delivered to you, ready for the next stage. **Outcome:** a full, finished draft of your book, completed to your satisfaction.

7. **Handoff to publication.** Your ghostwritten manuscript is ready for professional editing (often a final proofreading pass is sufficient since you've already reviewed every chapter), formatting, cover design, and publication. We can run all of those services in-house or hand off to your team.

## Confidentiality & Authorship

- **NDAs from day one.** Every member of the team signs a non-disclosure agreement before they see your manuscript. Your project, your identity, and your ideas remain confidential indefinitely.
- **100% authorship credit.** You are the author of the book. Period. Your name on the cover, copyright in your name, ISBN registered to you. The ghostwriter receives no public credit unless you choose to acknowledge them.
- **Full ownership of all material.** Every chapter, every character, every idea developed during the project is yours. We retain no rights to the manuscript or to derivative works.
- **No "ghostwriter portfolios."** We do not use ghostwritten projects in our portfolio without your explicit, written permission. Most authors prefer their projects remain private; we respect that completely.

**Can I narrate / write parts of my own book with your help?**
Yes — that's the Coaching & Writing model. You draft portions, your ghostwriter drafts others, and the team integrates them into a unified manuscript. The ghostwriter also provides feedback on your drafts and develops your voice. This is the most popular model for memoirs and thought-leadership books where the author's voice matters most.

**What level of research will you do for my book?**
The base level depends on your engagement model and genre. Standard fiction includes light research (period accuracy, terminology, cultural touch points). Complex fiction and non-fiction include moderate primary research. Deep research is available as add-ons (Sourcing Citations & Securing Permissions for non-fiction; Worldbuilding Bible Creation for fiction). For projects requiring extensive original research (interviews, archival work), we scope research as a separate engagement.

**What are the steps involved in the ghostwriting process?**
Vision mapping → strategic story structuring → ghostwriter match → chapter-by-chapter drafts → feedback & revisions → final manuscript delivery → handoff to publication. Detailed in the Process section above.

**How do you handle technical or industry-specific books?**
Books in technical, medical, legal, scientific, or specialized professional domains are matched with ghostwriters who have direct experience in or formal training in those fields. We also engage subject-matter consultants for fact-checking when needed. Specialized non-fiction is priced as Complex Non-Fiction reflecting the additional craft required.

**Are your ghostwriters native speakers of my book's language?**
Yes — our primary roster writes in native English. We also have ghostwriters who write in Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and Mandarin. For other languages, we can usually source a writer through our network; please ask about availability for your target language.

**What happens to unused ideas or content developed during the project?**
Everything generated during the project — including outlines, character sketches, abandoned chapters, and research notes — belongs to you. If we draft material that doesn't make the final book, you still own it. We retain no rights and don't reuse any of your material in other projects.

**Will the book include a suggested marketing or branding strategy?**
Marketing strategy is not included in standard ghostwriting, but several add-ons connect to it: Book Proposal Development for non-fiction (which includes a marketing plan), Comparative Title Analysis (in our Editing service), and Query Letter Review. For full marketing support, see our Marketing & Promotion service — many ghostwriting clients bundle the two.

**Can you provide writing samples before I hire your team?**
Yes. We can share anonymized samples in your genre from past projects (with NDAs respected — only samples where the original client granted release). For specific writers we're considering matching you with, we can provide unattributed samples of their published work in similar genres.
