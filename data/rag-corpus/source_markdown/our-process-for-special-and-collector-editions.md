# Special & Collector Editions Process

A special edition succeeds when scarcity meets genuine quality. We design the object, confirm the specification against samples, and structure the release so demand is captured without over-committing the print run.

Concept & Specification: Define the type of edition, its features, and its scarcity, and confirm the specification against the author's vision.

Design & Sampling: Develop the design and produce material samples or proofs so the object is tangible before the run.

Pricing & Release Plan: Set pricing and a release structure — including optional pre-order or crowdfunding — tuned to the author's audience.

Production: Coordinate the premium print run, including special bindings, finishing, and packaging.

Fulfillment & Numbering: Handle numbering, signing logistics, certificates, and fulfillment of the limited run.

Follow-Through: Support the launch and capture learnings for future editions.
