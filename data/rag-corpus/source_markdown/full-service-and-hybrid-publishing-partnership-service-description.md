# Full-Service & Hybrid Publishing Partnership

## Overview

Beyond individual services, BookCraft offers ongoing publishing partnerships for authors who want a committed partner across the full life of their books rather than a one-off project. We offer two partnership models. In Full-Service Self-Publishing, we act as your end-to-end publishing and growth partner while you retain ownership and rights. In Hybrid-Assisted Publishing, BookCraft shares a portion of production and launch costs in exchange for a defined revenue share, for authors who want a commercially engaged partner while preserving capital. Both models are built around a single backlist or catalog and a long-term growth plan, with transparent terms agreed before anything moves forward.

## Core Offerings

Full-Service Self-Publishing: BookCraft manages the transition and ongoing operations — production, design, platform setup, metadata, distribution, and growth — while you retain full ownership and rights. Recommended when a catalog has already demonstrated quality and reader trust and the priority is professional, growth-focused management.

Hybrid-Assisted Publishing: BookCraft subsidizes a meaningful portion of production and launch costs in exchange for a defined revenue share. Designed for authors who want a commercially engaged partner while preserving capital, and especially compelling when active institutional or specialty sales are part of the plan.

Catalog Performance Review: A title-by-title review of an existing catalog's platform data and performance to establish a baseline and identify the highest-leverage opportunities before committing to a model.

Title-by-Title Growth Strategy: A concrete, prioritized growth plan for each book — critical-priority titles needing immediate action, high-potential titles, and focused opportunities — translating research into a roadmap.

## Optional Add-Ons

Backlist Optimization: Refresh of covers, descriptions, metadata, and categories across an existing backlist to lift discoverability and conversion.

In-Development Title Launches: Launch strategy for new or in-development titles into an established backlist, so new books benefit from existing audience and momentum.

Institutional & Specialty Sales: Targeted outreach into institutional, professional, and specialty channels where a catalog's subject matter has a natural audience.

Implementation Roadmap & Reporting: A phased implementation plan with clear milestones and transparent performance reporting throughout the partnership.

## Ideal For

Authors with an existing catalog who want a long-term publishing and growth partner rather than a single project.

Authors transitioning a backlist in-house who want ongoing management after the move.

Authors deciding between retaining full control (Full-Service) and sharing cost and risk for a defined revenue share (Hybrid-Assisted).

Authors whose work has demonstrated quality and reader trust and is ready for focused, professional growth.

## Deliverables

A catalog performance review and a recommended partnership model with transparent terms.

A title-by-title growth strategy with prioritized actions and a phased implementation roadmap.

Ongoing management of production, design, distribution, and growth under the agreed model, with regular reporting.

A clear written agreement defining ownership, revenue model, responsibilities on each side, and how the partnership is reviewed and renewed.
