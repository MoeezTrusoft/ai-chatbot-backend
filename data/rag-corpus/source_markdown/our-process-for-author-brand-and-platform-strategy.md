# Author Brand & Platform Strategy Process

Building a platform is a compounding effort. We start with the foundation — who you are to readers — then put repeatable systems in place so growth continues between book launches.

Discovery & Brand Audit: Understand your goals, genre, existing audience, and current channels, and audit what is already working.

Brand & Positioning: Define your author positioning, voice, and visual identity.

Channel & Growth Strategy: Design the owned-channel plan and the audience-growth tactics tuned to your genre.

Systems Setup: Set up or optimize the newsletter, website touchpoints, and social content systems.

Activation: Launch the growth and engagement plan with sequences and a sustainable content cadence.

Measure & Refine: Track results against the metrics that matter and refine the plan over time.
