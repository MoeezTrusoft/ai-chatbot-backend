# Translation & Foreign-Rights / Localization

## Overview

Our Translation & Foreign-Rights / Localization service helps authors reach readers in other languages and markets. We coordinate professional literary translation, adapt the book's design and metadata for each target market, and help you navigate foreign rights — whether you want to license translation rights to international publishers or self-publish localized editions yourself. The goal is a faithful, market-ready edition in each language, not a literal word swap: tone, cultural references, and reader expectations are all considered.

## Core Offerings

Professional Literary Translation: We match your book with experienced literary translators who work into their native language, preserving voice, tone, and meaning rather than translating mechanically.

Localization & Cultural Adaptation: Beyond translation, we adapt cultural references, idioms, measurements, and conventions so the book reads naturally to its target audience, while respecting your intent.

Localized Metadata & Positioning: Titles, descriptions, keywords, and categories are recreated for each market — not just translated — so the book is discoverable and well-positioned where it is sold.

Foreign-Rights Guidance: We help you decide between licensing translation rights to an international publisher and self-publishing localized editions, and we keep your rights clearly mapped so nothing is double-committed.

## Optional Add-Ons

Edition Redesign for Market: Cover and interior adjustments where a market expects a different visual convention, while keeping the brand recognizable.

Multi-Market Distribution Setup: Configuration of localized editions across the right regional platforms and retailers.

Translation Quality Review: An independent second-translator review of accuracy and readability for high-stakes titles.

Rights Tracking: A clear record of which rights are licensed, to whom, in which territories and languages, and for how long.

## Ideal For

Authors with a successful book who want to reach readers in additional languages and markets.

Authors approached about foreign rights who want guidance on whether and how to license.

Authors who want to self-publish localized editions rather than license to a foreign publisher.

Authors of nonfiction or genre fiction with clear international audience potential.

## Deliverables

Professionally translated and localized manuscripts in each target language.

Market-ready metadata and, where needed, adapted cover and interior design for each edition.

A clear foreign-rights map showing what is licensed or self-published, where, and in which languages.

Localized editions configured for distribution in their target markets, with ownership retained by you.
