__Author Brand & Platform Strategy__

__How is this different from your Marketing & Promotion service?__

Marketing & Promotion drives a specific campaign or launch. Brand & Platform Strategy builds the long-term foundation underneath it: your author identity and the owned channels — newsletter, website, social — that let you reach readers directly for every future book. They work well together, but one is a sprint and the other is the infrastructure.

__I don't have an audience yet. Is it too early?__

No — that is often the best time to start. Building a platform early means your audience is growing while your book is being written or prepared, so you launch into a warm following rather than from a standing start.

__Do you guarantee a certain number of followers or subscribers?__

We do not guarantee specific audience numbers — anyone who does should be treated with caution. We build the brand, systems, and growth plan that reliably compound over time, and we report transparently on list growth, engagement, and conversion so you can see the progress.

__Will you run my social media for me day to day?__

This service focuses on strategy, systems, and setup. We can build content frameworks and templates that make ongoing posting sustainable for you, and day-to-day execution can be scoped as an add-on or handled through our Marketing & Promotion service.

__How does this connect to my website and newsletter?__

Your website and newsletter are the core of an owned platform. We integrate the brand and growth strategy with them directly, and can coordinate with our Author Website service to make sure the site is built to capture and convert readers.

__Who owns the accounts and audience we build?__

You do. Everything we set up — your newsletter list, social accounts, and website — belongs to you, with full access and ownership, so your audience is a durable asset you control.
