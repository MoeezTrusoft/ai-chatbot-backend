# Catalog Transition & Rights Recovery

## Overview

Our Catalog Transition & Rights Recovery service helps authors who already have published books move their catalog from a former, closing, or current publisher into their own publishing operation — cleanly, respectfully, and without losing their sales history. Whether your publisher is closing, your contract is ending, or you simply want to take control of your backlist, we manage the entire operational transition: recovering rights and source files, migrating platform accounts, rebuilding metadata, and reconciling royalties. We handle the logistics so you keep your readers, your reviews, and your income stream intact under your own imprint.

## Core Offerings

Rights & Chain-of-Title Review: We gather and review the contracts, rights, and chain-of-title records for each title to establish exactly what was granted, to whom, and on what terms — the foundation for a clean transfer back to you.

Platform & Account Migration: We plan and execute a continuity-preserving migration across retail and distribution platforms (Amazon KDP, IngramSpark, Barnes & Noble Press, Apple Books, Kobo, Google Play Books, and audiobook platforms), so listings keep their history rather than being rebuilt from scratch where possible.

ISBN, Copyright & Metadata Rebuild: We handle ISBN transfers or new registrations, coordinate with the relevant agencies, and rebuild metadata, keywords, categories, and enhanced retail content so each book is properly anchored and discoverable under your imprint.

Production Asset Recovery: We collect and verify manuscripts, interior layouts, cover source files, and other production assets so your titles can be reprinted or updated without rebuilding from scratch — and store them securely under your ownership.

## Optional Add-Ons

Royalty Reconciliation & Financial Close-Out: We organize final royalty accounting and financial records from the prior publisher for a clean reconciliation and your tax records.

Third-Party & Collaborator Coordination: We coordinate with designers, illustrators, editors, and co-authors whose information can be passed along, so valued working relationships continue.

Sub-License & Foreign-Rights Mapping: We map any foreign rights, audio rights, or other sub-licenses that may have been extended to third parties, so nothing is lost or double-committed in the move.

Re-Launch & Backlist Refresh: Once the catalog is under your control, we can refresh covers, descriptions, and positioning and coordinate a re-launch to re-energize the backlist.

## Ideal For

Authors whose publisher is closing, has closed, or is winding down, who need to continue their catalog without interruption.

Authors ending a contract who want to bring their backlist in-house and retain full rights and control.

Multi-title authors who want a single partner to manage the operational complexity of moving an entire catalog.

Authors who want the transition handled cooperatively and respectfully, preserving relationships with their former publisher.

## Deliverables

A complete, organized record of rights, contracts, ISBNs, metadata, and production assets for every title in the catalog.

Live, migrated retail and distribution listings under your own imprint, with sales history and reviews preserved wherever the platforms allow.

All publishing accounts, credentials, and source files transferred into your ownership and secure storage.

A clean financial reconciliation and a documented handover sign-off confirming the transition is complete.
