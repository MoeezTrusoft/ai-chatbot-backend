# Translation & Foreign-Rights / Localization Process

A great localized edition reads as though it were written in its language. We pair the right translator with each market and treat metadata and design as part of localization, not an afterthought.

Market & Rights Assessment: Identify the most promising languages and markets and clarify which rights you hold and want to use.

Translator Matching: Match the book to native-language literary translators with relevant genre experience.

Translation & Localization: Translate and culturally adapt the manuscript, with author touchpoints on key choices.

Metadata & Design Adaptation: Recreate metadata and adapt design for each target market.

Quality Review: Review accuracy and readability before release.

Distribution & Rights Tracking: Set up distribution for each localized edition and maintain a clear rights record.
