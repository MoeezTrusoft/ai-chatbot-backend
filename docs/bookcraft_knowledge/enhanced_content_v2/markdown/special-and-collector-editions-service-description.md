# Special & Collector Editions

## Overview

Our Special & Collector Editions service creates premium, limited, and gift-worthy versions of a book for authors who want to reward their most engaged readers and capture additional value from an existing title. From signed and numbered limited runs to deluxe hardcovers, boxed sets, and anniversary editions, we design and produce special editions that feel like objects worth owning. This service suits authors with a proven title or a devoted following who want a higher-margin, scarcity-driven product alongside their standard edition.

## Core Offerings

Limited & Numbered Editions: Signed, individually numbered runs with certificates of authenticity — scarcity products for an author's most devoted readers.

Deluxe & Special Bindings: Elevated production: cloth or leather-effect binding, foil, debossing, sprayed or stained edges, ribbon markers, and premium stock.

Boxed Sets & Series Collections: Custom slipcases and boxed sets that package a series or related titles into a single collectible the buyer wants to own in full.

Anniversary & Commemorative Editions: New editions of an existing title with fresh design, bonus material, or a new introduction to re-energize sales and mark a milestone.

## Optional Add-Ons

Archival Print Inserts: Signed art prints or archival inserts that add collectible value to a limited edition.

Custom Packaging & Unboxing: Designed packaging and presentation that make the edition feel like a gift and a shareable unboxing moment.

Bonus Content: New forewords, annotations, sketches, or behind-the-scenes material exclusive to the special edition.

Pre-Order & Crowdfunding Support: Structuring a limited edition as a pre-order or crowdfunding campaign to gauge demand and de-risk the print run.

## Ideal For

Authors with a proven title and a devoted following who want a premium, scarcity-driven product.

Series authors who want to package their books as a collectible boxed set.

Authors marking an anniversary or milestone for an existing title.

Authors who want a higher-margin, gift-worthy edition alongside their standard book.

## Deliverables

A designed and produced special edition to the agreed specification (limited, deluxe, boxed set, or commemorative).

Certificates of authenticity and numbering for limited runs, where applicable.

Production-ready files and coordination of the premium print run.

A pricing and release plan for the edition, with optional pre-order or crowdfunding structure.
